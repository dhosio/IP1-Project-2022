First catamaran on the island!

If there is a place more beautiful than the island of Mauritius, it would be the large ocean that surrounds it. With miles and miles of nothing but beautiful waters to transverse, it is trully remarkable what one gets to see when touring on a catamaran. For a very affordable amount, one is able to get a full day experience on this vessel and tour the waters of Mauritius accompanied by friendly faces and excuisite on-board food.

Hiking to the highest point!

There is more to see on the Island of Mauritius than the sandy beautiful beaches. To the south of the island, is a beautiful place called Le Morne. It is a historic place that is monumental to the people of Mauritius as it holds key memories of a place where people lost their lives at the hands of colonialists. But in contrast, there is a beautiful beach that accompanies this monumental location to brighten the mood.
